Sweet Mandazis

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder (or 1/2 tsp baking soda)
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom (optional)
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (or regular milk/water)
  • 2 tbsp butter or margarine (melted)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Add the egg, melted butter, and coconut milk gradually. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead for about 5 minutes, then cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll dough to about 1/2 inch thick and cut into triangles or squares.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry pieces until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve warm.

Notes

  • Use coconut milk for authentic flavor.
  • You can add grated coconut for extra taste.
  • Best served warm with tea or coffee.
